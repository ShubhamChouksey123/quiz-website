Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

How do I take a quiz?

Simply click on "Start Quiz" button in the navigation menu. You'll be presented with multiple-choice questions. Answer all questions and submit to see your results!

How does the leaderboard work?

After completing a quiz, your score is automatically added to the leaderboard. Higher scores rank higher on the board. Compete with others to reach the top!

Can I retake quizzes?

Yes! You can take quizzes as many times as you want. Each attempt helps you learn and improve your knowledge on various topics.

Is it mobile-friendly?

Absolutely! Quiz Wizz is fully responsive and works seamlessly on all devices - desktop, tablet, and mobile phones.
